═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LOCATION-BASED POLLUTION ALGORITHM:

1. HIMALAYAN REGION (Lat > 30°)
   - Clean mountain air
   - PM2.5: 15-35 µg/m³
   - AQI: 1-2 (Good to Moderate)
   - Examples: Kashmir, Shimla, Dehradun

2. NORTH PLAINS (Lat 25-30°)
   - High pollution
   - PM2.5: 80-150 µg/m³
   - AQI: 3-4 (Unhealthy)
   - Examples: Delhi, Chandigarh, Lucknow

3. CENTRAL INDIA (Lat 20-25°)
   - Moderate pollution
   - PM2.5: 50-80 µg/m³
   - AQI: 2-3 (Moderate)
   - Examples: Bhopal, Nagpur, Mumbai

4. SOUTH INDIA (Lat < 20°)
   - Better air quality
   - PM2.5: 30-60 µg/m³
   - AQI: 1-2 (Good to Moderate)
   - Examples: Bangalore, Chennai, Kerala
