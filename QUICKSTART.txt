🎨 AQI COLOR CODING:

🟢 AQI 1 - Good (Green)
   Air quality is satisfactory

🟡 AQI 2 - Moderate (Yellow)
   Air quality is acceptable

🟠 AQI 3 - Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups (Orange)
   Sensitive groups may be affected

🔴 AQI 4 - Unhealthy (Red)
   Everyone may experience health effects

🟣 AQI 5 - Very Unhealthy (Purple)
   Health alert for everyone

Problem: Port 5000 already in use

Problem: Module not found error
Solution: Run: pip install -r requirements.txt

Problem: Model not found
Solution: Run: python model_trainer.py

Problem: Browser shows "Can't connect"
Solution: Make sure app.py is running in terminal
