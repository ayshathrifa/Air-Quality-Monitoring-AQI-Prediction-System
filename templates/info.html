📚 AQI Information & Health Tips

Understanding Air Quality Index

🎨 AQI Categories

🟢 AQI 1 — Good

PM2.5: 0–12 µg/m³

Air quality is satisfactory, little or no risk.

🟡 AQI 2 — Moderate

PM2.5: 12.1–35.4 µg/m³

Acceptable. Sensitive individuals may be at risk.

⚠️ AQI 3 — Sensitive Groups

PM2.5: 35.5–55.4 µg/m³

Sensitive groups may experience health effects.

🚨 AQI 4 — Unhealthy

PM2.5: 55.5–150.4 µg/m³

General public may experience health effects.

🔴 AQI 5 — Very Unhealthy

PM2.5: >150.4 µg/m³

Health alert: risk increased for everyone.

💨 Pollutants Explained

PM2.5: Fine particles ≤2.5 micrometers. Can penetrate deep into lungs.

PM10: Particles ≤10 micrometers. Causes respiratory issues.

CO: Carbon Monoxide. Reduces oxygen delivery to organs.

NO2: Nitrogen Dioxide. Irritates airways.

O3: Ozone. Causes breathing problems.

SO2: Sulfur Dioxide. Affects respiratory system.

🏥 Health Tips by AQI Level

Good (1-2)

  • Perfect for outdoor activities
  • Enjoy exercise and sports
  • Open windows for fresh air

Moderate to Unhealthy (3)

  • Limit prolonged outdoor exertion
  • Children and elderly stay indoors
  • Consider wearing a mask

Unhealthy (4)

  • Avoid outdoor activities
  • Wear N95 mask if going out
  • Close windows and doors
  • Use air purifiers indoors
  • Asthma patients keep inhalers ready

Very Unhealthy (5)

  • STAY INDOORS!
  • Avoid all outdoor activities
  • Use N95/N99 masks if must go out
  • Keep windows closed
  • Run air purifiers on high
  • Vulnerable groups consult doctors

👥 Vulnerable Groups

These groups are more sensitive to air pollution:

  • 👶 Children and infants
  • 👴 Elderly people (65+)
  • 🫁 People with asthma or respiratory diseases
  • ❤️ People with heart disease
  • 🤰 Pregnant women
  • 🏃 Outdoor workers and athletes

🛡️ Protection Measures

  • ✅ Wear N95/N99 masks when AQI is high
  • ✅ Use air purifiers with HEPA filters
  • ✅ Keep indoor plants (natural air purifiers)
  • ✅ Avoid smoking indoors
  • ✅ Monitor AQI regularly
  • ✅ Stay hydrated
  • ✅ Eat antioxidant-rich foods
