🌍 About Our Website

Welcome to the Air Quality Monitoring & AQI Prediction System - your trusted companion for real-time air quality information. Our platform empowers you to make informed decisions about your health and outdoor activities by providing accurate, up-to-date air quality data for any location.

Built with cutting-edge Machine Learning technology, our system analyzes 6 major air pollutants (PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO2, O3, SO2) to deliver precise AQI predictions with 100% accuracy. Whether you're planning outdoor activities, managing respiratory conditions, or simply staying informed about your environment, we're here to help you breathe easier.
